iCargo Module

Rate Engine

Rate Engine was the pricing and surcharge configuration module used to manage carrier accounts, services, rate setup, and customer pricing behavior in the logistics platform.

Why It Matters

This is a strong business-rules story. Pricing modules are valuable because the complexity is in conditions, account-service combinations, and surcharge behavior rather than simple CRUD.

Tech Stack

  • AngularJS admin module
  • carrier and service configuration
  • rate-card and surcharge rules
  • platform pricing workflows

What I Worked On

  • carrier account and service configuration
  • rate setup and pricing rules
  • surcharge-rule management
  • admin-side pricing workflow maintenance
